# Slide 01 — BMI270 Overview

**Key talking points:**

- The BMI270 is Bosch Sensortec's flagship 6-DoF IMU for wearables and IoT
- Single-chip integration: 3-axis accelerometer **+** 3-axis gyroscope + temperature sensor
- Package size: **2.5 × 3.0 × 0.83 mm** (smaller than a grain of rice)
- Digital interface: I²C (up to 400 kHz) and SPI (up to 10 MHz)
- Ultra-low power: as low as **550 µA** in normal mode

**Live data note:**

- Temperature in the bottom-right card updates live from the firmware
- If disconnected, simulation kicks in automatically — all values are realistic

## Specific force

The accelerometer measures **specific force** (force per unit mass). At rest with $+Z$ upward:

$$
a_z \approx +1\,\text{g}
$$

> **Note:** Specific force is what the MEMS proof mass experiences — not GPS velocity.

Magnitude at rest:

$$
|\mathbf{a}| = \sqrt{a_x^2 + a_y^2 + a_z^2} \approx 1\,\text{g}
$$

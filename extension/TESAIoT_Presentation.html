PSoC Edge AI & Four On-Board Sensors
09:30–10:00 · 30 min · Infineon PSoC™ Edge: CM55 (ML/inference) + CM33 (connectivity/RTOS)
SHT40
Air temperature (°C) + relative humidity (%RH)
I²C · scalar · slow update rate
DPS368
Barometric pressure (hPa) + chip temperature
Weather / altitude clue applications
BMI270
6-axis IMU — accel (m/s²) + gyro (°/s)
Fused orientation: pitch · roll · yaw
BMM350
3-axis magnetometer (µT) + chip temperature
Compass-style applications
Every reading = name + number + unit. Vector strength = √(x²+y²+z²).
